# Deal With Notimess

Notification-message, Notimess, which I named are those messages come as notifications. And I think that it is the most part of the information we get everyday.

**How to deal with it?**

You may ask: "Deal with it? Why should I deal with it? It just as usually as eating or sleeping in our daily life."

Well, today, Internet creates massive messages from the world, which may overwhelmingly control our lives. Unconsciously, people are sucked in those disorganized information, and loss their directions in this real world.

To be honest, that is my pain at this moment, and also a specific chance for me to write this blog.

In morning, it's easy to open my eyes and phone, and check updates on x, youtube, github, telegram, discord or other social medias. Gradually, I don't even know who am I, what I want to do. And when I readed those notimesses, I just feel that my brain is almost a blank, a void return value. And there may left some agitation at most. Why there is agitation? Because I'm angry about there is no more updates for me to entertain.

The result is that, I just know I must wake up, go for breakfirst, and continue my job at my working position. I'm sinking day in and day out, with no hope and any power to go forward.

「君たちはどう生きるか」、宮崎さんが言ってた。でも、その言葉は俺にはすごく辛いものです。俺は、どこに行くべきかわからない迷い子です

Many days passed, I noticed a fact: "The situation seems a little strange, I think it's time to change it".

So, what I found is to make some definitions and rules before getting or receiving any notimesses, here it is:

**Definitions:**
1. Notimesses are messages, objective existences
2. Human are subjective existences
3. Messages type 1: The gold that I want to search, find, confirm, driven by my curiosity. (Maybe 1%)
4. Messages type 2: The staff that is a little interesting, which I want to collect for after-reading. (Maybe 19%)
5. Messages type 3: The rubbish that should not be read more than 1 sec. (Maybe 80%)

**Rules:**
1. I, as a human, must choose the messages initiatively, which I really want to pay attention to.
2. Spend 80%, 19% and 1% of my time in type 1, 2, 3 message.
3. Dig out the first and second rules and execute, after I lose myself and bury the rules I made into the grave.

So, as an experiment, let me have a try to test it.

That's all, no results yet, but maybe later.
